**Dominic Desjardins** is a Canadian former ice hockey right winger who played in the Farm Games from 1983 until 2004. He currently holds the all-time points record in Farm Games history with 1,876 points in a 21 year long career. He played for the [Cleveland Red Roosters](cleveland_red_roosters), Salt Lake City Crusaders, [Brandon Jacks](brandon_jacks) and [St. John's Mariners](st__john_s_mariners). He is currently the coach of the [Portland Patriots](portland_patriots).

Dominic Desjardins is the younger brother of the ill-fated Philippe Desjardins, older brother of Mario Desjardins, Paul Desjardins and Don Louis Desjardins, and father to Antony Desjardins and Xavier Desjardins. He is a no-nonsense man who hates jokes and laughter, and had a contentious relationship with everyone in his family. He is twice divorced and prefers the company of burly men, for reasons unknown. He once physically assaulted Pete Larsgaard, apparently in retaliation for Larsgaard planting bugs and cameras in the Desjardins home while Larsgaard was host of The Beef Stove.
