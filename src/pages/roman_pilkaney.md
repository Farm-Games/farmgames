**Roman Pilkaney** is a former ice hockey professional centerman who played from 1993 until 2010. Drafted by the [Kentucky Colonists](kentucky_colonists), he had a break-out season in 1994 with 23 goals, and performed well in their 1995 [Denley Cup](denley_cup) run. Upon leaving Kentucky, he played for the Regina Dutchman, Kamloops Bohemians, [Calgary Jailbirds](calgary_jailbirds) and Tulsa Tomahawks.
