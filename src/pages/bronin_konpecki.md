**Bronin Konpecki** is a former centreman ice hockey player in the Farm Games ice hockey league. He played from 1979 until 1999 for the [Newmarket Roadrunners](newmarket_roadrunners), Houston Giants and [Portland Patriots](portland_patriots). He was well respected around the league for his calm and collected attitude, but undying passion for cum and hot-cold play. During the mid-to-late 90's, Bronin and [Flame Crennelchovik](flame_crennelchovik) were an unstoppable pair on the Portland Patriots' first line, culminating in a 1998 Denley Cup Championship.
